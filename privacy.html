Privacy Policy

Software

My privacy policy is very simple: none of the software I develop collects any information about their users. Ever. Never has, never will.

Server Logs

Standard Apache2 server logs are kept for this site that include your IP address, the date and time you accessed the site, what files you have accessed and what browser you have used. These logs are retained for one year and then discarded. For instance, your reading of this file will have produced a log entry that looks something like this:

192.168.20.118 - - [26/May/2019:15:22:12 +0800] "GET /privacy.html HTTP/1.1" 200 1127 "-" "Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10_14_5) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/74.0.3729.157 Safari/537.36"
